I am an Advocate of the Republic of Uganda, based in Kampala — navigating the space where law, enterprise, and ideas converge. My practice is shaped by precision, and my ventures by a belief that distinctive experiences are always worth building.

Beyond the courtroom and the boardroom, I am a writer in progress. This site is my witness stand — where I document ventures, travels, reflections, and the long arc of a life lived with intention.

I believe that how you do anything is how you do everything. That principle runs through my legal work, my businesses, and the way I tell stories.
