About

A Builder
of Things That Last

M

Add your photo here

I am based in Kampala, Uganda — and I am hungry for change. Not the kind spoken about in conference rooms, but the kind built in the ground, deal by deal, venture by venture, word by word.

I move across law, esports, indigenous sport, and technology — not because I am scattered, but because Uganda's transformation will not come from one lane. It will come from people who refuse to be defined by a single discipline and who build wherever there is ground to stand on.

"Uganda — and all of Africa — is a green paradise and a promised land. What she needs most is for her people to believe in themselves."

This site is the only place in the world where the authentic Maurice lives on the page. No performance, no highlight reel — just the honest record of a journey being built in real time, from Kampala, for the world.

I am guided by faith and grounded in the belief that how you do anything is how you do everything. That principle shapes every venture I build, every argument I make, and every word I write.
