The Person

Rooted in principle,
drawn to possibility

Your portrait here

I am an Advocate of the High Court of Uganda — trained to interrogate, argue, and build from first principles. But the courtroom is only one theatre I operate in.

I am philosophically idealist and pragmatically rooted. I believe institutions matter, that language is architecture, and that good ideas die without execution. So I execute.

Through Cheza Kwepena, I'm building a bridge between East Africa's youth and its digital future. Through Nondescript Gaming Africa, I'm making the continent a player — not just a market. Through iStartApp, I'm helping ideas become businesses.

This site is where I think out loud — through journal entries, through images, through honest reflection on what it means to build something that lasts.
